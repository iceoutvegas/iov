About Us

Mission

Ice Out Vegas is a youth-led political activist group made up of students across the Clark County School District. We aim to amplify the voices of teenagers who want to reclaim their futures and bring real positive reform to our community.

Here are the current issues our work is focusing on:

  • Petition for the removal of Lorena Biassotti from the CCSD School Board
  • Petition for the relocation of U.S. World Cup matches
